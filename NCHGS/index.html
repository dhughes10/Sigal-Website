Welcome to the Society.

Since 1906, members of NCHGS have collected, preserved and showcased art, culture and American history made in Northampton County. View permanent and special exhibitions at our flagship Sigal Museum in historic downtown Easton. Research genealogy or family history at the Jane S. Moyer Library and the NCHGS Archives. Or join us at our three historic house properties for special events, or by appointment.
